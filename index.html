A new way to generate power. It's pretty cool.

Our turbine soars 600m above the earth to generate cheap, clean, and reliable energy from the wind. Using a balloon filled with lifting gas, our turbine can reach high winds above the earth to generate a lot of power in a small package.

Think green energy is too expensive? Think again.

Our turbine strikes at the heart of wind turbine costs: huge gearboxes, massive blades, and collosal towers. Our technology allows us to produce clean power at a radically lower price.

Cheap, clean, reliable power. It's a definite win.

Our turbines will fly for around five years, and are designed to hassle-free and easy to use. We want wind power to have the user-friendliness of solar power at the price of coal.
